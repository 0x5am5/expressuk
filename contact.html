Velvet Trumpet

Velvet Trumpet are a South London-based theatre company with a passion for comedic new writing.

Please visit our website for more information on the company and reviews of previous productions. www.velvettrumpet.co.uk


General Enquiries

As an emerging company we are always looking to form new relationships with equally passionate and talented theatre-makers.

If you are a budding director, writer, stage manager, technician or designer interested in getting to know more about our work and/or would like to get involved in future projects then you can contact us at info@velvettrumpet.co.uk.

Please also get in touch with any general enquiries about performance dates, times and locations or if you would like to make a comment, suggestion or complaint.

Press Enquiries

If you are a venue, reviewer, agent or member of any other professional organisation looking to get in touch then please email Samuel at samuel@velvettrumpet.co.uk.
