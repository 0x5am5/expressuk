News!

Exciting news this week! Will Thomas is officially a local celebrity. He is about to become the youngest driver ever to reach a MILLION MILES for Express UK, thus earning himself the prestigious 'Golden Gloves' award. Will is to be interviewed this week by Route One magazine and the Evening Post are even coming down to take some photos of our local hero.

Will has been with us since he was 20! And we're so proud to have him operating our fleet to London every day. Will is due to earn his millionth mile on his way to London this Friday so unfortunately won't be home but we will most certainly celebrate when he arrives back here in Swansea. For those of you lucky enough to have a ticket for the 09:30 Swansea-London service on Friday, complimentary party poppers and crisps will be served.

(Party poppers are not to be fired whilst the vehicle is in motion. Please wait until you get to Reading Services between junctions 11-12).
